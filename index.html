FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Best Sign for My Shop or Service?

The best sign depends on your specific needs and location. We can help you evaluate the best options, considering visibility, budget, and branding goals.

How Much Does a Sign Cost?

Sign costs vary based on size, materials, and complexity. We provide free quotes after an initial consultation to understand your needs.

How Long Will It Take to Have a Sign Ready?

The timeline depends on the sign type and our current workload. We typically deliver within a few weeks after design approval.

Do You Fix Light or Replace Lamps on an Existing Sign?

Yes, we offer light repair and replacement services for existing signs to keep them looking their best.

What is the Best Size for a Sign for My Shop?

The best size for a sign depends on the distance and speed from which your sign will be viewed. We'll help determine the best size for maximum impact.

Can I Order My Sign by Phone?

You can start the ordering process by phone but a face-to-face consultation is recommended to finalize the order.

Do You Accept Credit Cards or PayPal?

Yes, we accept credit cards and PayPal for your convenience.

How Long is Your Warranty?

We offer a 1-year warranty on our products. Warranty may vary by product.
